How it works

Simple from your end. Handled on ours.

KET takes the complexity and stress out of shopping and procurement. Here is exactly what happens from the moment you reach out.

No matter what you need — a market run, a procurement job, gift sourcing, or vendor validation — our approach stays the same.

1

You don't have to go to the market, chase vendors, or manage logistics. We handle all of that while you focus on your day.

Step by step

What happens after you reach out.

Here is the process from start to finish — no guessing, no surprises.

1

Tell us what you need

Reach out via our contact form, WhatsApp, or by calling us directly. Describe your request — what you're looking for, any specific details, quantities, preferences, or timeframes that matter to you.

You don't need to have everything figured out. Even a rough description is enough for us to start.

💬 Preferred channels: WhatsApp (+234 810 157 1161) or the contact form on our website.
2

We review and confirm your request

Once we receive your message, we review your request and follow up to confirm details — pricing expectations, availability, and any specifics we need to do the job right.

If something is unclear, we'll reach out. We'd rather ask one question up front than get it wrong later.

⏱ We aim to respond as soon as possible. For urgent requests, please call us directly.
3

We source and coordinate

This is where KET does the work. We go to the market, locate reliable vendors, validate suppliers, compare options, and coordinate the procurement process on your behalf.

We treat your request the same way you would — with care, attention, and a focus on getting you quality at the right value.

✅ This applies across all our services — market runs, procurement, vendor validation, gift sourcing, and more.
4

You get the best — without the stress

The result: you receive what you need without having gone through the process of finding it. No traffic, no unreliable vendors, no wasted time.

That's the KET promise — we go through the stress so you can get the best.
