About KET

We help you shop and procure without the stress.

K
Who we are

A shopping agency built for busy people in Lagos.

KET Shopping Limited is a shopping and procurement agency based in Lagos, serving busy individuals who value their time and want convenience without compromising on quality.

We understand how exhausting it can be to navigate Lagos markets, track down reliable vendors, or simply find the time to handle errands. That's exactly the problem we exist to solve.

Whether you need someone to handle a market run, source a specific product, validate a vendor, or guide you through purchasing decisions — KET handles it so you don't have to.

What we do

We handle the parts of shopping
that slow you down.

From market runs to vendor validation, KET takes on the time-consuming, stressful parts of shopping and procurement so you get results without the hassle.

Market Runs

We handle your market errands so you don't have to deal with traffic, crowds, or unpredictable delays. Tell us what you need and we take care of it.

Procurement

We help source and purchase items based on your needs, ensuring you get what you're looking for at the right quality — without the back-and-forth.

Market Pickup & Vendor Validation

We verify vendors before you commit and handle pickups on your behalf, so you avoid unreliable suppliers and unnecessary risk.

Market Guidance & Product Survey

We help you understand your options, pricing, and what's available in the market before you buy — so you make better, more informed decisions.

Our purpose

Why KET exists.

We understand how time-consuming and unpredictable shopping and procurement can be — especially in Lagos. Traffic, unreliable suppliers, confusing options, and wasted hours are all too familiar.

KET exists to take that burden off you and make the process easier, faster, and more reliable — so you can spend your time on what actually matters.
